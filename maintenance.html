Bike Care Tutorials

Lesson 1

Lubricate the Chain

Use a dry lubricant for dry conditions and a wet lubricant for wet/muddy conditions. Apply a drop of lubricant to each link while slowly pedaling backward. Let it sit for a few minutes, then wipe off the excess with a clean rag.

Lesson 2

Check the Gears

Shift through all gears to ensure smooth transitions. Adjust the derailleur if shifting is slow or noisy. Lesson 3

Maintain the Tires

Check for proper tire pressure and inflate to the recommended PSI. Inspect the tread for wear and replace tires if they are bald or cracked. Patch or replace the inner tube if there's a puncture.

Lesson 4

Replacing a Bike Chain

Lay the old chain next to the new one to match the length (if the old chain is the correct size). Use a chain breaker tool to remove excess links.
